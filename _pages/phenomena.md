*Under construction.*

A pokédex of phenomena I find interesting but can't yet explain — either because no good explanation exists, the existing ones feel incomplete, or I'm working one out myself. My hunch is that they're all shadows cast by the same high-dimensional hull — pieces of one jigsaw we haven't finished assembling.

Silhouettes mark mysteries still open, credited to the context where I came across them. Full images mark phenomena that have since been understood.
